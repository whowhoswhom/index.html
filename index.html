Intel's Innovation Timeline

1971

The Birth of the Microprocessor

Intel introduced the world's first commercial microprocessor, the 4004, revolutionizing computing and laying the foundation for the digital age.

1985

386 Processor Launch

The Intel 386 processor brought 32-bit computing to personal computers, enabling more powerful software and multitasking capabilities.

2006

Core Architecture Revolution

Intel Core processors introduced multi-core technology, dramatically improving performance while reducing power consumption.

2023

AI-Powered Future

Intel's latest processors integrate advanced AI capabilities, enabling intelligent computing at the edge and accelerating machine learning workloads.
